Who we are

We are a small, independent AAA game development studio based in Franconia, Germany. We love playing video games of all kinds, but we love making games even more. That's why our mission is to create immersive mobile games that captivate and delight players on all mobile operating systems. We aim to eliminate boundaries and ensure that our games can be played seamlessly on any mobile device, providing players with an unforgettable experience, regardless of their choice of device. We strive to develop games that not only entertain, but also stimulate the imagination and allow players to dive into captivating worlds.

Games

Our studio is currently working on our first major project, named "Project Cast Away." Simultaneously, a second, confidential project is in an advanced stage of development. We are excited to bring our visions for these projects to life and strive to provide our players with an unforgettable gaming experience.
