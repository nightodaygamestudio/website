WE ARE NIGHTODAY

An independent game studio based in Franconia, Germany. We love playing video games of all kinds, but we love making games even more. That's why our mission is to create immersive games that captivate and delight players. We focus on developing AAA mobile-first games but we also keep an open mind to the possibility of releasing our games on other platforms in the future, possibly including board games. Our goal is to eliminate boundaries and ensure that our cross-platform games can be played seamlessly and provide players with an unforgettable experience, regardless of their choice of device, operating system or control mechanism. We strive to develop games that not only entertain but also stimulate the imagination and allow players to dive into captivating worlds.

GAMES

Our studio is currently working on our first major project, codenamed "SPELLFIRE." Simultaneously, a second confidential project is in an advanced stage of development. We are excited to bring our visions for these projects to life and strive to provide our players with an unforgettable experience. Join our Discord for announcements and sneak peeks.
