・About Us・

We are a small, independent AAA game development studio located in Franconia, Germany. Our mission is to create immersive mobile games that captivate and engage players on all mobile operating systems. We strive to eliminate boundaries and ensure that our games can be played seamlessly on any mobile device, offering players an unforgettable experience regardless of their device choice.

・Games・

Our studio is currently working on our first major project, named "Project Cast Away." Simultaneously, a second, confidential project is in an advanced stage of development. We are excited to bring our visions for these projects to life and strive to provide our players with an unforgettable gaming experience.
